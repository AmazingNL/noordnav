Welcome to Amsterdam Noord

Getting around Amsterdam Noord is really easy. You can enjoy the free ferry ride, use the bus, or take the underground metro, with your ticket or Ov-chipcard to explore different parts of the city. Explore the fun and exciting neighbourhood of Amsterdam Noord. It’s a really cool place where old history meets new ideas. Whether you’re checking out awesome art, experiencing local culture, or tasting some delicious food, there’s always something to do. With so many ways to travel, your movement around Amsterdam Noord will be simple easy and fun!

Ferry Terminal

Ferry Schedules: Look at the ferry times to see when the boats go. The free ferries take you across the water to NDSM the Noord Amsterdam and places like the big train station Amsterdam Central. They come often, so you don’t have to wait too long. You can see the times at the dock or on your phone. Riding the ferry is fun and easy.

Bus Station

Bus Routes: Get around the city easily with buses. They take you to popular spots like parks, museums, shopping places, and other fun areas. Buses have regular stops, so you can hop on and off as you explore. They come often, so you won’t have to wait too long for the next one. You can see where the bus will stop by checking the signs at each station or looking on your phone. Buses are comfy, safe, and a great way to see more of the city while traveling.

Metro

Metro Lines: Use the metro system to easily travel around the city! It’s fast, so you can get to where you want to go quickly. The metro takes you to lots of important places, like museums, parks, and shopping centers. You can catch the metro at stations all over the city, and it comes often, so you don’t have to wait for a long time. The metro is also really simple to use just buy a ticket or use your OV-chipcard, tap it at the station, and hop on! It’s a fun and easy way to travel around without getting stuck in traffic.

Ticket stand

Ticket Information: It’s easy to buy tickets and travel cards for your trips! You can get a single ticket if you’re only going somewhere once, or you can buy a travel card like the OV-chipkaart if you plan to take lots of trips. The OV-chipkaart is super helpful because you can put money on it and use it again and again! You can buy tickets from machines at the metro or bus stations, or even use your phone to get them on the GVB app.
