HOw to get to Amsterdam Noord

How to get to the Amsterdam Noord metro station:

To get to the Amsterdam Noord metro underground Station from the Amsterdam central station look for signs directing you to Amsterdam Noord follow the direction of the sign or ask any police officer or security or people around, you can get there by bus, bike, or walking. Buy a ticket or use your OV-chipcard. Tap your card at the entrance to check in, then go to the M52 metro platform that takes you to Amsterdam Noord. always check the Digital screen on the station to know when the next metro will come.

When you enter the metro you need to monitor the digital map that displays the next stop and also check your map too, the metro stops at each station for the passengers to drop and others to enter. From Amsterdam Central to Amsterdam Noord there are only two stops which is Noordapark and the last stop is Amsterdam Noord Station. This link takes you to the GVB Metro time table.
